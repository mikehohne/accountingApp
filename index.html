About the Firm

Sonora CPA Group Inc was founded in 1984 by Patti Hohne, joined by Bob Hoyt in 1999 and Jared Holt in 2015. In order to accommodate partnership changes, avoid the mouthfull of Hohne, Hoyt, and Holt, and make our name MUCH easier to spell, Sonora CPA Group Inc became our officially adopted firm name in early 2017. Based in Sonora, California, Sonora CPA Group Inc serves individuals, businesses, and trusts throughout the Central Sierras by providing tax and accounting services. We customize our services to fit the clients’ needs while making customer service a priority.
